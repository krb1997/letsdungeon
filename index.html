Characters

Creating a character that fills the persona of one's vision is both an exciting and a daunting task. Creation that breaks the ordinary yet embaces the immersive setting of the DM's world. A character detailed with backstory, strengths and weaknesses, providing a deep look into their personality. A character portrayed with stunning art conveying their style, tastes and culture. Its a challenge... One helped with soild tools, well detailed material and a sprinkle of creativity.

Writing their past

The Players Handbook - An indispensable tool bound with quick build guides, recommendations and ideas to help elevate your character to new heights.

Painting their style

HeroForge - Creating a 3D model of your character has never been easier. Export as VTT tokens or even bring your hero into the material plane.
